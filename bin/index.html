RIGHT SIDE OF THE ROAD

In Britain, drive on the LEFT.
In USA, drive on the RIGHT.
To switch lanes, CLICK or press SPACE.

Can you guess which nation you are by the word?

WHITE sign: Oncoming traffic in opposing lane.
BLACK warning: Obstacle in lane of travel.

Too easy?  Press SHIFT+2 to double the speed.
Too hard?  Press SHIFT+3 to halve the speed.
Press SHIFT+1 to return speed to normal.

Press number 0 to mute sound.

A game made in two days by Duncan Robson and Ethan Kennerly
for Ludum Dare 26 (April 26 to 28)
